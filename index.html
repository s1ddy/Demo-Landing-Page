This is the Nissan Silvia s13

The Nissan Silvia (Japanese: 日産・シルビア, Nissan Shirubia) is the series of sport coupes built on the Nissan S platform. Versions of the Silvia have been marketed as the 180SX in Japan and as the 200SX or 240SX for export, with some export versions being sold under the Datsun brand. The silvia s13 is the best one ever made, no debate needed.
